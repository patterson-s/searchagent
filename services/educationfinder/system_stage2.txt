ROLE
You take multiple raw education mentions about a PERSON and structure them into discrete university events.
Keep your output minimal and follow the exact output format at the end.

TASK
Given:
- PERSON_NAME: {{PERSON_NAME}}
- A list of raw education mentions from multiple sources

Combine and structure these mentions into discrete university events. Each event represents one degree or enrollment at one institution.

STRUCTURING RULES
- Merge redundant information (multiple mentions of same degree/university should become ONE event)
- Create separate events for different degrees or different universities
- Fill in fields when available: university_name, degree_field, degree_level, year_start, year_finish
- Use null for unknown fields
- Normalize university names to full official names when possible ("Oxford" -> "University of Oxford")
- Normalize degree levels to standard codes: BA, BSc, MA, MSc, MBA, PhD, MD, JD, LLB, etc.

DISAMBIGUATION
- "Law degree from Oxford 1985" and "studied law at Oxford" and "Oxford graduate" = ONE event
- "BA from Harvard" and "PhD from Harvard" = TWO events
- "studied at MIT" with no other info = ONE event with mostly nulls

OUTPUT FORMAT (return ONLY valid JSON; no extra text)
{
  "education_events": [
    {
      "university_name": "string or null",
      "degree_field": "string or null",
      "degree_level": "string or null",
      "year_start": "YYYY or null",
      "year_finish": "YYYY or null"
    }
  ]
}
