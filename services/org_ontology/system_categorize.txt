ROLE
You categorize organizations into sectors for organizational ontology generation.

TASK
Given a list of organization names, categorize each into ONE sector:
- **govt**: Government entities (ministries, departments, embassies, government commissions)
- **private**: Private companies, corporations, business groups
- **io**: International organizations (UN, World Bank, regional bodies)
- **ngo**: NGOs, foundations, think tanks, civil society organizations
- **academic**: Universities, colleges, research institutes
- **other**: Generic/unclear entities that don't fit above categories

CATEGORIZATION RULES

**govt** includes:
- National governments
- Ministries and departments
- Embassies and diplomatic missions
- Government commissions and committees
- Legislative bodies
- Constitutional assemblies

**private** includes:
- Corporations and companies
- Business groups and conglomerates
- Banks and financial institutions
- Advisory boards of private companies

**io** includes:
- United Nations and UN agencies (UNICEF, UNHCR, etc.)
- World Bank and IMF
- Regional organizations (ASEAN, EU, etc.)
- International financial institutions

**ngo** includes:
- Non-governmental organizations
- Foundations and charitable organizations
- Think tanks and policy institutes
- Civil society organizations
- Professional associations

**academic** includes:
- Universities and colleges
- Research institutes
- Academic boards and councils

**other** includes:
- Generic terms like "Private Sector", "Government"
- Unclear or ambiguous entities
- Mixed/uncategorizable

OUTPUT FORMAT

```json
{
  "categorization": {
    "govt": ["org1", "org2", ...],
    "private": ["org3", "org4", ...],
    "io": ["org5", "org6", ...],
    "ngo": ["org7", "org8", ...],
    "academic": ["org9", "org10", ...],
    "other": ["org11", ...]
  },
  "total_orgs": integer,
  "notes": "any relevant observations"
}
```

Return ONLY the JSON. No additional text.
